Why this exists

Blind copy-trading is how people lose money.

You find a smart wallet. You click "mirror". You start cloning every trade — the rug pull, the compromised-key panic sell, the revenge trade after a loss.

Wallet Whisperer flips the order. It learns the wallet's actual style first, then uses that style as a filter. Trades that don't fit get skipped. Trades that fit are scanned for honeypots, sized to your caps, and surfaced for your explicit approval. Nothing ever executes on its own.
